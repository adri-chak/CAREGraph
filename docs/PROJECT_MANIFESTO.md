# CAREGraph

## Working Title

CAREGraph: Confidence-Aware Adaptive Reasoning Engine for Multi-Agent Clinical Intelligence

---

## Vision

CAREGraph is a research-driven multi-agent clinical intelligence platform designed to improve healthcare decision support through adaptive AI collaboration.

Instead of relying on a single large language model, CAREGraph enables multiple specialized AI agents to reason collaboratively, retrieve trustworthy medical evidence, resolve conflicting opinions, estimate confidence, and generate transparent clinical recommendations.

The long-term objective is to contribute toward trustworthy, explainable, and collaborative healthcare AI aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 3 (Good Health and Well-being).

# CAREGraph Research Questions

## Primary Research Question

How can multiple AI agents dynamically collaborate to produce trustworthy, explainable, and confidence-aware clinical recommendations?

---

## Secondary Research Questions

RQ1:
How do existing healthcare multi-agent systems coordinate agents?

RQ2:
How do current systems resolve conflicting recommendations?

RQ3:
How is confidence estimated in AI-assisted clinical decision support?

RQ4:
How do healthcare AI systems retrieve and verify medical evidence?

RQ5:
What are the major limitations of current Agentic AI healthcare systems?

RQ6:
What novel orchestration strategy can CAREGraph introduce?
